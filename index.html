WHO ARE WE?

Flower Power Festival is a vibrant celebration of peace, love and culture from the 60s and 70s. Born from a desire to revive the spirit of an iconic era, our festival brings together people of all ages to enjoy music, art and community. With each edition, we transform the space into a place full of colors, joy and good vibes, where diversity and inclusion are at the heart of our celebration.

Through live performances, creative workshops and interactive activities, we seek to inspire our attendees to connect with their essence and celebrate life. Our commitment is to create a safe and welcoming environment, where everyone can freely express themselves and enjoy the positive energy that only Flower Power can offer. Join us and be part of this unique experience!
